How does this work? ▼
  • Solana addresses are random by default (e.g. 7bY3...).
  • This tool quickly generates thousands of valid keypairs per second until one matches your chosen prefix.
  • Your private key is generated safely — all computation happens on this machine, and no keys are ever saved or sent anywhere.
  • A match could be instant or take days depending on the length and your CPU speed.
  • Always back up your private key in a secure password manager. The private key controls all funds in this wallet — treat it like cash!
